🎸 DadRock Tabs

AI-powered guitar and bass lessons, bringing classic rock to musicians worldwide in 14 languages.

About the Project

DadRock Tabs was created by a 49-year-old guitarist with no formal programming experience and a passion for helping others learn guitar.

For years, I have been teaching classic rock and heavy metal songs to a community of more than 75,000 YouTube subscribers. I dreamed of building a website and Android app that could reach musicians all over the world in their own language, but without coding knowledge, that goal felt impossible.

During OpenAI Build Week, I used ChatGPT as my development partner to transform that vision into a real product.

Today, DadRock Tabs is a multilingual learning platform that helps guitarists and bassists discover songs, improve their skills, and learn classic rock through an experience available on both the web and Android.

- 🌍 Available in 14 languages
